Physics Wallah


"Nothing great ever achieved without enthusiasm"

From its humble beginnings as a YouTube channel launched by Alakh Pandey Sir in 2014, Physics Wallah has blossomed into a thriving community, now comprising over 6 million dedicated students.

Physics Wallah's mission is to furnish top-notch educational resources at an accessible price point, catering to the aspirations of JEE/NEET/NDA/GATE/UPSC hopefuls.

Physics Wallah stands as India's premier online educational technology platform, offering an affordable and comprehensive learning experience tailored for students from classes 6 to 12, as well as those diligently preparing for JEE and NEET exams. Additionally, the platform offers mock tests, crash courses, and a myriad of supplementary resources.

Alakh Pandey joined the field of education in the digital age, and rather than teaching college courses, he created a YouTube channel and used it to instruct pupils. Despite having only earned Rs 5,000 in tuition fees at beginning, he went on to develop a corporation valued at Rs 9100 crore.
